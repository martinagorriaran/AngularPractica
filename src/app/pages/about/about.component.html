This article is about the game's ENEMIES. For the more info on each foe, see the category page or FOE FACTS! for the achievement.

ENEMIES are creatures and entities that serve as obstacles that the player has to overcome in OMORI. Most of them can be battled against, meaning that enemies are not defined by being antagonistic.
